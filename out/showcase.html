How you type it

Pairs, joined by a pipe

Each column is the top character and bottom character joined with |. The font's ligatures fuse every pair into one stacked glyph.

1Install a .otf from out/ (double-click, then Install). Pick Sans or Grotesk, regular or condensed.
2Type pairs as TOP|BOTTOM, one per column.
3Turn on ligatures in your app (standard or discretionary).

To write HELLO over WORLD you type (no spaces between pairs):

H|WE|OL|RL|LO|D
